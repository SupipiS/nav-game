Hungry Bunny

Welcome to the bunny's world.

Help the bunny to find the carrot. On it's way to the carrot there are holes and grass. Your task is to feed the bunny as much grass you can. So you have to take the longest way to the carrot at the given time. If you fell into a hole, you will lose points. Direct the hungry bunny to the carrot while collecting points.


Good Luck and Enjoy!! 🐰🥕
